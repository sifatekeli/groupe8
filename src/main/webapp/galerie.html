Why We Are Different From The Rest

Our Services

Website Design

We understand that a website is important for any business and a brilliant design is vital to attract new people to your website.

We create designs that work well for your needs keeping the balance right to ensure users get a great visual experience as well as finding the information about your company as easily as possible.

Website Development

Behind all the fancy visual effects of any website lays the code that makes it all happen, and this is much more important than many realise.

We use the latest coding methods in our industry so that all of our websites have high performance to match the beautiful design.

Social Media

Social Media is a brilliant investment for any business large or small. You can gain a lot of new customers through social networking using services such as Facebook or Twitter. We can do all the hard work for you by getting your company setup with social media accounts so you don't have to worry.

Search Engine Optimisation

SEO is a technique that involves carefully looking at your website content, targeting keywords that people would use when searching for your services. We would research your business and aim to find keywords that suit your business and alter your content over a few months with the aim of moving your website higher in the rankings.

Content Writing

If you don't have the time to create the content for your website such as images or text, we can perform our content writing service for you. We would research your company & industry, producing high quality information for your business and transfer it to your website, doing all the hard work while you focus on your other commitments.

Responsive Design

Gone are the days where you own a second mobile version of your website. These days it is all about "Responsive Design" where you own one website that adapts to the users scree size, regardless of device. Using modern techniques we are able to make sure your website functions as intended from large screens all the way down to mobiles.

Content Management System

Maintaining a website can be a daunting task, especially if you don't understand coding. Content Management Systems allow you to edit your website such as add new pages and change content without the need for coding knowledge. As well as this you are able to make changes while your website is still live, leaving your website running undisturbed while for your visitors.

Custom Computer Construction

Buying a computer can be very confusing, especially if you are not sure what to get, or if you are getting a good deal at a high street shop. Having your computer custom built means you get a brilliant system with high quality parts throughout, instead of paying for a brand name with low end components. If you are not sure what you need, just give us an idea of what you want out of your computer and we will source the parts and build it all for you.
